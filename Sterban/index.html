Sterban

Sterban will be the official mainnet of the Sterban crypto ecosystem. It powers the entire ecosystem and processes transactions on the Sterban Virtual Machine (SVM), an extension compatible with the Ethereum Virtual Machine (EVM). Sterban will be an Optimistic Rollup that aims to feel exactly like interacting with Ethereum, but with transactions costing a fraction of what they do on L1(Etherium). Centralized Sequencer receives users transactions and regularly sends the transaction batch to mainnet Ethereum.
